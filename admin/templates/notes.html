Why this Course?

This is course is everything you need from start to finish regardless of experience. It is the only course like this online taught by a true bootcamp instructor. 94% of his students go on to get a web developer job. His bootcamps cost a lot of money, and his goal for this course was an online equivalent to these in person bootcamps. Very flexible. Everything is up-to-date and exciting technologies. This is the only complete developer course that covers NodeJS. This program is constantly updated with new content, projects, and modules.
